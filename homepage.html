How We Measure & Price Your Laundry

Priced by Kilogram (kg)

We use kilograms to measure general laundry loads like washing, drying, and folding. Ideal for regular clothes, bedsheets, towels, and daily wear.

Priced by Item (pcs)

We charge per piece for services like ironing, dry cleaning, or special garments. Best suited for shirts, suits, curtains, and delicate fabrics.

ℹ️ Note: Pricing by kg allows for more accurate billing and faster turnaround. No need to separate your items—we’ll do it for you!
⚠️ Reminder: Special or delicate items are treated individually. We'll notify you of any adjustments based on item type.
